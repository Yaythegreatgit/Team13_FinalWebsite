What are Esports?

Esports are a form of competition in video games. Esports often takes the form of organized, multiplayer video game competitions, particularly between professional players, individually or as teams

General Tips

Getting "good" at the game requires dedication, patience, and communication. Spending time to improve your own abilities is necessary and working on being a team player is just as important. A team player needs to effictevily communicate with his team in order to secure the objective.

When it comes to any game, there are four important things you should have, a decent computer that can run the game with smooth settings, this doesn't mean the computer must run the game at the best settings smoothly but the game should be playable. Since most time will be spent online facing opponents to get better, a good internet connection is important, trying to get better in a laggy environment helps no one. A decent keyboard/mouse is also useful. Anyone will work, but investing in a mechanical keyboard and a mouse with good response time can improve your play in these very competitive games. Finally, a headset is also advantageous, being able to communicate with your team is important so a decent mic and headphones will make communication much easier.

Rocket League (2015)

Rocket League is a competitive team battle where players face off in a street soccer style matchup in cars. The game has many modes including differing number of teammates, different ball and goal style, and a mode where players get special abilities. In the competitive scene, the most common game type is 3v3s.

Necessary Skills

  1. Mechanical skills- Rocket League leans heavily on mechanical ability, top players spend many hours perfecting mechanical prowess to master ball control in any situation. Playing goalie, dribbling on the ground or air, and passing and shooting all utilize mechanical skill the harder they get.
  2. Positional knowledge- Rocket league is a very fast paced game, knowing where to be at what time is the difference between a successful save or shot.

Things to Consider

  • Unlike the other esports, most rocket league pros use a controller instead of mouse and keyboard to excel at the game. Investing in a controller is highly recommended if you want to get into competitive Rocket League, most pros use dualshock 4 or 3 controllers or xbox controllers.
  • Visual settings have a big impact on the game, the angle that you see your car and the rest of the game will affect how you play. Finding a style that suits you is important, but for a start This link will show you the settings pros use which you can test out for yourself.

CS:GO (2012)

Counter Strike: Global Offensive, CSGO, or even more simply referred to as CS, is a competitive shooter. The most common game-type is competitive 5v5s, games are a best of 30 where teams play as the terrorists (ts) or counter-terrorists (cts). The terrorists win by eliminating the counter-terrorists or successfully planting the bomb on one of two bomb sites and preventing the counter-terrorists from defusing it before it can go off. The counter-terrorists win by eliminating the terrorists before the bomb is planted or by defusing a planted bomb.

Necessary Skills

  1. Mechanical Ability- Weapons have spray patterns to learn and grenades have many "lineups" to learn to tactically cutoff and outplay the enemy
  2. Game Sense- Experience in the game, knowing in this situation what are the likely moves of my opponents and how can I counter them effectively
  3. Map Knowledge- There are various competitive maps, that players need to know very well. This includes different materials, peek spots, boost spots

Important Settings

Changing in game settings to a style that suits you is imperative for any game. While you need to come up with your own settings, this link will show you the settings pros use when they play the game. Taking inspiration from those better than you is a great way to improve. This includes customizing your crosshair, changing the color, size, and style of your crosshair will affect the amount of vision you have in the most important part of the screen, where you are aiming.
